How to Get Here

✈️

By Air

The closest airports are:

  • JFK — 10 min from venue, ideal
  • LGA (LaGuardia) — ~30 min by car
  • EWR (Newark) — ~45 min by car
🚗

By Car

Russo's on the Bay is easily accessible via the Belt Parkway (Exit 17 – Cross Bay Blvd). Ample parking is available at the venue.

🚇

By Subway/AirTrain

Take the A train to Howard Beach–JFK Airport station. From there, it's a short cab or rideshare to the venue. The AirTrain also connects JFK to the subway.

🚖

Rideshare

Uber and Lyft are widely available throughout Queens and from all major airports. We recommend pre-booking for Saturday night reliability.

🗽

If you're visiting New York for the first time, consider arriving a day or two early to explore the city! Let us know if you'd like any local recommendations — we'd love to help you make the most of your trip.
