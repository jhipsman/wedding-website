How to Get Here

✈️

By Air

Several airports serve the area at a range of price points:

  • HPN (Westchester) — ~50 min from venue, often the most affordable option
  • SWF (Stewart International) — ~1 hr 15 min, budget-friendly with many low-cost carriers
  • EWR (Newark) — ~45 min by car, good selection of routes and fares
🚗

By Car

Russo's on the Bay is easily accessible via the Belt Parkway (Exit 17 – Cross Bay Blvd). Ample complimentary parking is available at the venue.

🚇

By Subway / AirTrain

Take the A train to Howard Beach–JFK Airport station. From there it's a short rideshare to the venue. The AirTrain also connects JFK to the subway system.

🚖

Rideshare

Uber and Lyft are widely available throughout Queens and from all major airports. We recommend pre-booking for a Sunday evening for reliability.

🗽

If you're visiting New York for the first time, consider arriving a day early to explore the city! Let us know if you'd like any local recommendations — we'd love to help you make the most of your trip.
